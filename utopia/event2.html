Cheesetasting

Michel Folley, French born and trained Master Fromager, will help you discover the world of cheese. France, with over 2,000 years of cheesemaking tradition, sets the gold standardfor fine fromages Over the past 20 years, Americans have become serious about cheesemaking.Come see what all the fuss is about!

Comparisons of the following:

  • Ash-ripened goat cheese from the Loire vs. Massachusetts Bonne Bouche.
  • The top dogs of both countries: Cheddar vs Comte.
  • Roquefort vs Wachusett Mountain Organic Blue Cheese.
